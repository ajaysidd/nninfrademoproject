About Company

NN Infra Developers, established on April 1, 2018, is a premier infrastructure development company based in Hyderabad. With expertise in open plot development and eco-resort projects, we focus on innovation, quality, and sustainability. Our notable projects, including Green Valley and Sree City Eco Resort & Farm Land, reflect our commitment to excellence. With a dedicated team and a strong freelancer network, we continue to shape Telangana’s real estate landscape.

About Us

At NN Infra Developers, we strive to create modern and eco-friendly developments that enhance communities. Operating from Uppal, Hyderabad, we specialize in open plots and sustainable resort projects. Our mission is to blend infrastructure with nature, ensuring high-quality living spaces. With a customer-centric approach and a vision for growth, we aim to redefine infrastructure development in Telangana.
